Yogurt Soup

This is a Turkish yogurt soup that is really delicious and easy to cook.
Ingredients
  1. 4 cups whole milk
  2. 3 cups plain yogurt
  3. 2 egg yolks
  4. 5 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  5. 2 cups water
  6. 4 teaspoons chicken bouillon granules
  7. 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  8. 1 teaspoon salt
  9. Â½ teaspoon ground black pepper
schedule
  1. Prep: 10 mins
  2. Cook: 25 mins
  3. Total: 35 mins
  4. Serving: 4
  5. Yield: 4 servings
Steps
  1. Beat milk, yogurt, egg yolks, and all-purpose flour in a large bowl with an electric mixer.
  2. Meanwhile, bring water and chicken bouillon to a boil in a large soup pot. Reduce heat to medium-low and add yogurt mixture, lemon juice, salt, and pepper, stirring occasionally until mixture thickens, about 20 minutes.
